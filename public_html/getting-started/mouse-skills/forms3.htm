  • The little boxes to the left are checkboxes.

  • They allow you to select one or more items from a list of options.

  • Select one or more of the desserts listed by clicking in the checkbox next to it.

  • Notice that a check mark will appear in the box next to each item you select.

  • Clicking the checkbox a second time removes the check mark and "unselects" the item.
