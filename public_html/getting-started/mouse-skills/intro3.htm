Introducing the Cursor

 

The cursor is an arrow or other symbol on the screen that moves as you move the mouse. It is usually an arrow, but its appearance will vary depending on whether it is over text, blank space, an image or a website link.

 

During this tutorial, the cursor will change from the arrow shape
 to a pointing finger
 to an i-beam
 
 or a blinking vertical line |

 
Press the "Enter" key on the keyboard to continue.
