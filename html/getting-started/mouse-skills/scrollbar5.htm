Scrolling

 

Horizontal scroll bars allows you to move the content left and right if the content is too wide to fit the screen. Find the horizontal scroll bar at the bottom of the window (in the area indicated by the moving hand). These horizontal bars work the same way vertical scroll bars do. Try the horizontal scroll bar now by either clicking and dragging with the left mouse button or by using the right and left arrow keys on the keyboard.
